Paper Cut-Out 1.13 quickfixw compiled by Masogir (masogir@gmail.com)
I take no credit in the development of Paper Cut-Out. All credit goes to superalgae: https://www.planetminecraft.com/member/superalgae/
I love Paper Cut-Out and wanted to release a quick fix for Minecraft 1.13 to keep it alive!

This pack contains the bare-bones minimum Paper Cut-Out textures from previous builds to make it work for Minecraft 1.13
It does not include the mcpatcher folder, alt textures, alt pvp textures, etc...
The "w" at the end of quickfix means that I (kind of) fixed the water textures. It's not perfect and superalgae still need to make it better.


For Developers, the following is a complete list of 1.13 textures (not included in this pack) that require retexturing (sorry if any spelling/typos):
